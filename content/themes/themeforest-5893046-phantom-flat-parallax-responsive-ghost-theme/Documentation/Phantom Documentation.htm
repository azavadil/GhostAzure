Instalation.

  • Open to your FTP client (or explore your ghost directory if you are hosted ghost locally).
  • Navigate to ghost/content/themes.
  • Paste main phantom folder.
  • Restart ghost and log in to your dashboard.
  • In settings under themes select phantom from fropdown menu. * Click save. That's all, now time to configure your theme.

Configuration.

For a full functionality of this template, you have to go the setup process. It will not take too long.

General functionality

  • Go to your dashboard and open settings page.
  • There fill inputs like blog name, description etc.
  • On blog logo you can add your logo, to be showed in footer and single post page.
  • On blog cover upload your image to be showed as your homepage cover photo. I used one of 4 covers included to this template.

Remember! If you wannt your homepage to look pretty, make sure that your cover image has no less than 1920px width and 700px height.

  • Into user section, fill areas and add your avatar. That info's and your avatar will be showed in a footer of every single post u write.

That's all from the front-end side. Now you only need to write some javascript's. (just kidding ;))

Features functionality

There are some features like second logo addicted to cover image, like you see phantom logo looks well in footer, but imagine how it will look on a cover image.

Featured Images

If you wannt to add featured background to single post, just write this code:
![cover]. Now, on preview side of your post will you see special place to with caption: "Add image for cover". Click on that or just drag image to this place from your local files.

Remember! If you wannt your single page to look pretty, make sure that your featured cover image has no less than 1920px width and 700px height.

Remember! Always put ![cover] after first paragraph, this first paragraph is automatically used on homepage as post excerpt. Quick Example from this post:

Footer social links

As you see in footer, there are 3 random buttons. If you wanntt to add social media to your theme, do it in order:
* Open phantom/partials/footer.hbs * Overwrite # with links to appropriate url's i.e if you wann't a link to your dribbble account, it should look like this:

<a href="http://dribbble.com/yourProfile" target="_blank"><i class="icon-dribbble"></i></a>

There are many link you can add, twitter, facebook, linkedin, everything you need is correct icon. Full list of icons included to this template you can find here.

Wann't facebook button? Let me explain in a example:
<li><a href="http://facebook.com/youProfile" target="_blank"><i class="icon-facebook"></i></a></li>

as you see i overwrited hashtag # and added special facebook icon icon-facebook from included icons list.
